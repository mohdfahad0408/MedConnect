General Questions

What is MedConnect? ‚ñº
MedConnect is a comprehensive healthcare platform that allows patients to easily book appointments with qualified doctors and healthcare professionals. We connect you with over 200+ doctors across various specialties, making healthcare accessible and convenient.
How do I create an account? ‚ñº
Click on the "Sign In" button in the navigation bar and select "Login as Patient." You'll find a registration option where you can create your account using your email address or phone number. The process takes less than 2 minutes!
Is MedConnect available 24/7? ‚ñº
Yes! Our platform is available 24/7 for booking appointments and managing your healthcare. While doctor availability varies by specialty and location, our customer support team is always ready to assist you with any questions or concerns.

Booking & Appointments

How do I book an appointment? ‚ñº
Booking an appointment is simple! First, choose your preferred doctor or specialty. Then, select an available time slot that fits your schedule. Finally, confirm your booking and receive instant confirmation with all appointment details via email and SMS.
Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment? ‚ñº
Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without any penalty. Simply go to "My Appointments," select the appointment you wish to modify, and choose the cancel or reschedule option.
What happens if I'm late for my appointment? ‚ñº
We recommend arriving 10-15 minutes before your scheduled time. If you're running late, please contact the clinic immediately. Depending on the doctor's schedule, they may still be able to see you, though you might experience a longer wait time.
How do I find a specialist? ‚ñº
You can browse specialists by department using our "Browse by Department" section on the homepage. We offer specialists in Cardiology, Neurology, Pediatrics, Orthopedics, Dermatology, Ophthalmology, Dentistry, and General Practice. Each department page shows available doctors with their qualifications and patient reviews.

Payments & Insurance

What payment methods do you accept? ‚ñº
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and digital payment methods including PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Some clinics also accept insurance - please check with the specific provider during booking.
Do you accept insurance? ‚ñº
Many of our partner doctors and hospitals accept insurance. During the booking process, you'll see which insurance plans are accepted by each provider. You can also filter search results to show only doctors who accept your specific insurance plan.
Will I receive an invoice for my appointment? ‚ñº
Yes, you'll receive a digital invoice via email immediately after your payment is processed. You can also download invoices anytime from your "My Appointments" section for insurance reimbursement or record-keeping purposes.

For Healthcare Providers

How can I join MedConnect as a doctor or hospital? ‚ñº
Click on "Sign In" and select "Login as Hospital/Doctor" to begin the registration process. You'll need to provide your medical credentials, license information, and clinic details. Our team will review your application within 48 hours and guide you through the onboarding process.
Is there a fee to list my practice on MedConnect? ‚ñº
Basic listing is free! We only charge a small service fee per completed appointment. There are no upfront costs, monthly fees, or hidden charges. You only pay when you successfully serve patients through our platform.
How do I manage my appointment schedule? ‚ñº
Once registered, you'll have access to a comprehensive dashboard where you can set your availability, block dates, manage appointments, view patient information, and track your earnings. The system is intuitive and can be accessed from any device.
